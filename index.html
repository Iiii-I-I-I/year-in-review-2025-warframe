2025 Year in Review

Happy New Year Tenno! 2025 was an amazing year for the Warframe Wiki and the Warframe community! With the support of Digital Extremes, Weird Gloop, the volunteer admin team, and you, the Warframe Wiki successfully forked to its new home at wiki.warframe.com. We saw an amazing boost in community engagement, with an influx of new editors, returning names, and organic word-of-mouth about the move. We cannot appreciate you all enough for the continued support as one of the biggest video game wikis in the world. We All Lift Together!

Traffic statistics

The Warframe Wiki has seen its biggest page view count yet after its move, with more than 140 million views in total for the year! In December, we reached almost one million pageviews daily with the launch of Warframe’s largest update: The Old Peace. Throughout 2025, the wiki has consistently experienced growth in viewership from 250,000 daily pageviews, up until 900,000 daily pageviews, showcasing strong community word-of-mouth on the interwebs.
